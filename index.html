About

I am currently pursuing a masters degree at the University of Michigan School of Public Health with the Department of Health Behavior and Health Education. Formerly, I worked as a medical librarian at SUNY Upstate Medical University, an academic research center. I am cultivating expertise in Health Communications.

I believe in the power of information to improve the health care decision making process. I believe that well organized and easily accessed information aids in understanding. I believe a beautiful design can engage the viewer, and allow for processing the information in digestible pieces. I believe a combination of understanding literacy levels and education tailored to the audience can help improve understanding, as well as contribute to changing health behaviors. I am interested in researching the areas where design, libraries and education, and public health promotion intersect.

Portfolio

The below images are a few selections from my portfolio of communications and design work. The below work was created during my tenure at Upstate Medical University. The targeted audience for the illustrated posters were patients and families of an outpatient cancer center, based on an article about common cancer myths from the National Cancer Institute. The square images were 5"x5" magnets targeted to students, and clinical staff working or studying at the hospital. The banners hang in the foyer leading to the library.
